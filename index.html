What We Offer

Inspection Services

Every inspection is thorough, objective, and documented. You'll walk away with a clear picture — no surprises.

🏠
Buyer's Inspection

The most important step before closing. We evaluate every major system — structure, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, roof, and more — so you can negotiate with confidence or walk away informed.

📋
Pre-Listing Inspection

Sellers benefit from knowing exactly what buyers will find. Identify issues before listing to avoid deal-breaking surprises and price your home with authority.

🔬
Mold Testing

Mold can be hidden, hazardous, and costly. We identify visible growth and conduct air quality sampling to give you a factual baseline before you buy or sell.

🏢
Commercial Inspections

Protect your investment in commercial property. We inspect office buildings, multi-unit residential, and light industrial — evaluating structural, mechanical, and safety systems thoroughly.

⚠️
Lead Paint Testing

Common in homes built before 1978. We test painted surfaces for lead hazards, giving buyers, sellers, and landlords a clear, documented result for peace of mind and compliance.

💧
Water Testing

Especially important for properties on well water. We test for bacteria, contaminants, and water quality issues so you know exactly what's coming out of the tap.

About Us

An Inspector Who Actually Knows Buildings

Affirm Home Inspections is built on a simple belief: the person buying or selling a home deserves the full truth about what they're dealing with — not a quick walkthrough and a long PDF no one reads.

With a background in HVAC and years of municipal code and permitting work, Andrew Marshall brings a level of mechanical and structural insight that goes well beyond the standard inspection checklist.

Based in Central Illinois and serving the wider region, Affirm is your local, independent option — no franchise fees passed on to you, no conflict of interest, just a straight answer on every property.
